Bio

Like is a Berlin-based trio drawing inspiration from acts like Slint, La Dispute, Alex G, and Black Country, New Road. Rooted in punk's off-shoots, they weave organic patterns that are both trancey and dancey. Over intertwining and diagonal lines, the narrator lifts everyday happenings into gothic realms."
